Home Staging in Greater Boston

A proven marketing tool that helps homes sell at a higher price.

This is what we do...

What IS staging?
Preparing a home with impeccable styling and functional design so your buyer can mentally move in.

So, who exactly is a stager?
An experienced professional who understands buyer demographics, rules of design, and specific real estate markets.

Do you really need to stage a home?
The Real Estate Staging Association conducts ongoing studies comparing time on market and sale prices for staged and not staged homes. The stats don’t lie. A staged home will sell faster and at a higher price.

Who is using staging services?

  • Real Estate Brokers and Agents
  • Builders and Developers
  • Private Home Sellers
  • Rental Property Owners
